Clean-Up and Greening Program

A Clean-Up and Greening Program is an initiative aimed at improving the environmental health and aesthetics of a community. It typically involves activities such as:

Waste Collection and Disposal: Organizing clean-up drives to remove litter and debris from public spaces.

Tree Planting: Planting trees and other vegetation to enhance green spaces and improve air quality.

Community Beautification: Implementing projects to enhance the visual appeal of the community, such as painting murals or creating community gardens.

Skills Training and Livelihood Program

A Skills Training and Livelihood Program is designed to equip individuals with the necessary skills and knowledge to improve their employment opportunities or start their own businesses. It may include:

Vocational Training: Offering courses in various trades and skills, such as carpentry, sewing, or computer programming.

Entrepreneurship Development: Providing training and mentorship to help individuals start and manage their own businesses.

Job Placement Assistance: Assisting participants in finding employment or securing business opportunities.

Health and Wellness Fair

A Health and Wellness Fair is an event that promotes health awareness and provides various health services to the community. It may include:

Check-ups: Health Offering free or discounted medical check-ups, such as blood pressure and blood sugar screenings.

Health Education: Conducting seminars and workshops on health topics like nutrition, exercise, and disease prevention.

Health Services: Providing access to health services like dental check-ups, vaccinations, and mental health counseling.

ABOUT US


Barangay San Lucas 1 is a vibrant community nestled in the heart of San Pablo City, Laguna. Known for its warm hospitality and rich cultural heritage, our barangay offers a peaceful and welcoming environment for its residents. We are committed to providing essential services, promoting community development, and fostering a strong sense of belonging among our residents.
